Let's take a look into Bob's life:


  • 1945 - Robert Nesta Marley was born in Nine Mile, Saint Ann Parish, British Jamaica.
  • 1963 - Bob started his music career and formed the group "Bob Marley & The Wailers."
  • 1965 - The Wailer's first album was released, titled "The Wailing Wailers."
  • 1971 - The Wailer's 2nd album was released, titled, "The Best of the Wailers."
  • 1973 - The group released 2 more albums this year, titled, "Catch a Fire" and "Burnin."
  • 1974 - The Wailer's broke up and Marley began his solo career. Bob released an album titled, "Natty Dread."
  • 1976 - Bob released album titled, "Rastaman Vibration."
  • 1976 - Marley survived an assasination attempt in Jamaica.
  • 1977 - Album "Exodus" was released.
  • 1977 - Bob was diagnosed with Acral Lentiginous Melanoma.
  • 1981 - Bob died at age 36 from Skin Cancer in Miami, Florida.
  • 1984 - The greatest hits album "Legend" was released and became the best selling reggae album of all time.
